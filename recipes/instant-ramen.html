Instant Ramen

Ingredients

  • Two packages of instant ramen
  • Pre-cooked meat matching the flavor of ramen
  • Chopped carrots
  • Chopped green onion
  • One egg

Steps

  1. Add water to a pan and bring to a boil
  2. Add noodles and flavor packet to the pan and stir
  3. Add your egg and meat
  4. Let cook for 2-3 minutes
  5. Add your vegetables
  6. Let it all continue to cook untill the egg is fully cooked through
